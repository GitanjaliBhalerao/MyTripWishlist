Tokyo,Japan

With 2019’s Rugby World Cup kicking off in September and the Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympics fast approaching, this is the moment to plan a trip to the Japanese capital. More than 200 Michelin-star restaurants grace this metropolis, with culinary specialities including sushi, ramen and tempura. Experience the world’s most efficient public transportation system, which includes 13 subway lines and over 100 surface routes, and take in the unrivalled fashion of this fast-paced city. One thing to do in Tokyo?
Check out the neon lights of Shinjuku at dusk.
